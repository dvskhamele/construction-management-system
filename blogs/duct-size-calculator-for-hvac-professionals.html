Duct Size Calculator for HVAC Professionals

Proper duct sizing is a critical aspect of designing an efficient and effective HVAC system. The size of the ducts affects the amount of air that can be delivered to each room, the velocity of the air, and the overall performance of the HVAC system. Incorrectly sized ducts can lead to a number of problems, including poor indoor air quality, uncomfortable temperatures, and high energy bills.

The Importance of Proper Duct Sizing

Oversized ducts can result in low air velocity, which can lead to poor air distribution and stratification. Undersized ducts, on the other hand, can cause high air velocity, which can result in noise, increased energy consumption, and inadequate airflow. A duct size calculator is an essential tool for HVAC professionals to ensure that ducts are sized correctly for the specific requirements of each project.

Our Duct Size Calculator is simple to use. Just enter the following information:

  • Friction Loss: The friction loss in inches of water per 100 feet of duct.
