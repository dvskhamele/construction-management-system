Stay Organized: New RFI/Submittal Tracker Lite Chrome Extension!

Managing Requests for Information (RFIs) and Submittals is a critical part of keeping construction projects on track. Delays in these processes can lead to significant project setbacks and cost overruns. Our new RFI/Submittal Tracker Lite Chrome Extension is designed to provide a simple, accessible way to log and monitor these essential project communications.

Effortless Tracking for Your Project Communications

This extension allows you to quickly add, categorize, and track the status of your RFIs and Submittals directly from your browser. It's a lightweight tool to help you stay on top of important documentation without the need for complex project management software.

How to Use the RFI/Submittal Tracker Lite Extension

Once installed, click on the extension's icon. You can add new items by providing a description, selecting whether it's an RFI or Submittal, and setting its initial status (Open, Pending, Closed). The items are saved locally, and you can clear them as needed.

Key Features:

  • Log and track RFIs and Submittals.
  • Categorize by type (RFI/Submittal) and status (Open/Pending/Closed).
  • Simple and intuitive interface.
  • Local storage for quick access.
  • Clear all items function.
