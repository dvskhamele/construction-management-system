Introducing the Construction Defect Tracker: Streamline Quality Control

Maintaining high quality and addressing issues promptly are critical for successful construction project delivery. BuildMate is pleased to announce our new Construction Defect Tracker, a simple yet powerful tool to help you manage and resolve project defects efficiently.

Why a Defect Tracker is Essential

Effective defect management leads to better project outcomes, reduced rework, and increased client satisfaction. Our new tool helps you to:

  • Systematically log and categorize all identified defects.
  • Assign responsibility and set due dates for resolution.
  • Monitor the status of defects from identification to resolution.
  • Improve overall quality control and handover processes.

How Our Tool Works

The Construction Defect Tracker provides an intuitive interface to record key information about each defect:

  • Description: A detailed explanation of the issue.
  • Location: Precise location of the defect on the jobsite.
  • Severity: Categorize defects as Low, Medium, or High to prioritize.
  • Assigned To: Specify the individual or team responsible for the fix.
  • Due Date: Set a target date for resolution.

You can easily add new defects, view a list of all logged issues, and mark them as resolved as they are addressed.

Get Your Defect Summary Report

After logging your defects, you can generate a summary report. This report provides an overview of total defects, resolved vs. pending issues, and a breakdown by severity, helping you to quickly assess project quality status. Enter your email on the results page to receive a detailed report.
