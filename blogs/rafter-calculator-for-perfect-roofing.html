Why is a Rafter Calculator Important?

Guesswork has no place in construction. An inaccurate rafter measurement can lead to a series of problems, including:

  • Wasted materials and increased costs.
  • An unstable roof structure.
  • Leaks and other structural damages over time.
  • An uneven and aesthetically displeasing roofline.

Our Rafter Calculator eliminates these risks by providing precise measurements based on your project's specific dimensions.

How to Use the Rafter Calculator

Using the tool is straightforward. You only need two key measurements:

  1. Run: This is the horizontal distance from the outside of the wall to the center of the ridge board.
  2. Pitch: This is the slope of the roof, typically expressed as a ratio of rise over run (e.g., 4/12). You just need to enter the first number of the ratio.

Once you input these values into the calculator, it will instantly provide you with the exact length of the rafter required.
