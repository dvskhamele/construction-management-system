Real-Time Weather Data for Your Construction Sites

This extension provides quick access to current weather conditions and forecasts for your project locations. Simply enter a city or zip code, and get instant updates on temperature, humidity, wind speed, and more, helping you make informed decisions.

How to Use the Weather Overlay Extension

Once installed, click on the extension's icon. Enter the city or zip code of your project site, and click 'Get Weather'. The popup will display the current weather information, allowing you to plan your day accordingly.
