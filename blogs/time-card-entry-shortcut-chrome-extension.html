Streamline Your Time Tracking

This extension allows you to quickly input project names and hours worked, storing them locally for easy reference. It's designed for on-the-go professionals who need a simple, efficient way to keep a record of their time.

Once installed, click on the extension's icon. Enter the project name and the hours worked, then click 'Log Hours'. Your entries will be saved and displayed within the extension's popup. You can clear all logs when needed.
