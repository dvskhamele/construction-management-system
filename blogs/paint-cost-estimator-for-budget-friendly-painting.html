Our estimator provides a quick and precise way to calculate the total paint cost based on your project's specifics.

How to Use the Paint Cost Estimator

Our Paint Cost Estimator is designed for simplicity and accuracy:

  1. Enter Total Paintable Area: Input the total square footage of the surfaces you plan to paint (e.g., walls, ceilings).
  2. Specify Number of Coats: Enter the number of paint coats you intend to apply (typically 1 or 2).
  3. Define Paint Coverage per Gallon: Input the estimated coverage rate of your chosen paint in square feet per gallon (found on paint cans).
  4. Enter Cost per Gallon: Input the price of one gallon of your selected paint.
  5. Estimate: Click the "Estimate Paint Cost" button to instantly get the estimated gallons of paint needed and the total paint cost.

Understanding Your Results

The calculator first determines the total paintable area, factoring in the number of coats. It then calculates the total gallons of paint required and multiplies this by the cost per gallon to give you the total estimated paint cost. This clear breakdown helps you budget effectively and make informed purchasing decisions.
