Why Roof Pitch Matters

Roof pitch, expressed as a ratio (e.g., 6/12), represents the vertical rise of the roof for every 12 inches of horizontal run. Itâ€™s vital for:

  • Material Selection: Many roofing materials are designed for specific pitch ranges.
  • Drainage: Proper pitch ensures efficient water and snow runoff, preventing damage.
  • Aesthetics: Pitch significantly impacts the architectural style of a building.
  • Safety and Accessibility: Steeper roofs can be more challenging and dangerous to work on.

How to Use the Roof Pitch Calculator

Our Roof Pitch Calculator is user-friendly and provides quick, accurate results:

  1. Enter Rise: Input the vertical distance (in inches) from the top of the wall to the peak of the roof.
  2. Enter Run: Input the horizontal distance (in inches) from the edge of the roof to a point directly below the peak (half the span).
  3. Calculate: Click the "Calculate Pitch" button to instantly get the roof pitch ratio and its corresponding angle in degrees.

Understanding Your Results

The calculator will display the pitch as a ratio (e.g., 6/12), which means for every 12 inches horizontally, the roof rises 6 inches. It also provides the roof angle in degrees, which is helpful for various architectural and engineering considerations. This data empowers you to make informed decisions for your roofing projects.
