Milestones provide a structured way to break down large projects into manageable segments. A dedicated tracker helps you:

  • Improve Accountability: Assign responsibility for milestone completion.
  • Enhance Communication: Provide clear and concise updates to clients and team members.
  • Boost Motivation: Celebrate achievements as milestones are completed.
